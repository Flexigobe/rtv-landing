“We returned 45 faulty boots to the supplier and they just disappeared — no record, no idea if we ever got credited.” If you buy wholesale and hold your own stock, you know this hole. It's real money walking out the door with no audit trail.

What it does

RTV against the PO

Raise a return tied to the original purchase order, pick a reason (faulty / wrong / over-shipped), and keep it all linked.

Debit note + correct stock

Auto-generate the debit note (what the supplier owes you) and decrement the right location's inventory — no manual fiddling.

Chase the credit

Track each claim — open / awaiting credit / closed — until the supplier actually pays you back. Nothing disappears again.
